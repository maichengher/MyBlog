Welcome to my silly life

When I was a little girl I wanted to be an artist. When I turned 12, I wanted to be a teacher. When I turned 16, I wanted to own my own business. And when I went to college, I wanted to help people. Since then I've been searching for myself...not sure of which way to go. Today, I am asked 'What do I want the world to see me as?' And I replied, I want to show the world how beautiful life is. So here I am, promting myself, and promting my own vision. Life is beautiful. and we all should live it bravely.
